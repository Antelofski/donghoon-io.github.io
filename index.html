Donghoon Shin

I am an HCI researcher and a Ph.D. student in Human Centered Design & Engineering at the University of Washington, where I am co-advised by Profs. Gary Hsieh and Lucy Lu Wang. I will be joining Adobe as a research scientist intern this summer

My research interests lie at the intersection of AI and translational science. More specifically, I design and build AI-infused interfaces to support evidence-based and deliberate practices in real-world contexts, such as design and healthcare

Before joining UW, I did my undergrad in ECE and Information Science at Seoul National University, where I was honored to be selected as the Presidential Science Scholar. I also worked as a research intern at Naver AI and an iOS developer at Elecle

I am the recipient of the 2022 Korean Government Scholarship for Overseas Study and Honorable Mention Award at CHI 2020
